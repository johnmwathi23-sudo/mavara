About Mavara Safaris

We are a Kenyan-owned tour & travel company, experts in safaris, tours, holiday planning, flights, hotels, and adventure travel.

Our name "Mavara" means spots or patterns, symbolizing the beauty, diversity, and unique markings of wildlife such as leopards and cheetahs. Our team consists of travel experts with real experience and deep knowledge of Kenya’s wildlife and culture.

Mission

To deliver unforgettable, safe, and personalized travel experiences.

Vision

To be Kenya’s most trusted safari partner.
